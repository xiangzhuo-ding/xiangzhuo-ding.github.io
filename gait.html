Gait Analysis Based on Deep Learning

Gait Analysis Based on Deep Learning

In this project, we present a Recurrent Neural Network classifier model that segments the walking data recorded with instrumented footwear. The signals from three piezoresistive sensors, a 3-axis accelerometer, and Euler angles are used to generate temporal gait characteristics of a user. We were able to merge all sensor data from the instrumented footwear without any data preprocessing, or any human intervention, to generate gait characteristics. This greatly reduces the processing time and makes the technology amenable for real-time applications.
