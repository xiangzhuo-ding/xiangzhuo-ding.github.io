Intelligent Electric Self-Balancing Scooters

In 2016, two students and I started a program designing intelligent multiple-functional electric self-balancing scooter. The system can transform from self-balancing scooter into tricycle expediently. The modeling and analysis practice within the program, which was completed mainly by CAD and CAE software, helped us successfully realize 3D modeling. Finally, we came up with a foldable structure by using multiple mobile parts, which has both the function of self-balancing scooters and tricycle without any compromise on its performance. And as a result, a national award and a patent were achieved.
